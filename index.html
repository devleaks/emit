Variable Explanation Value Simulation
Vehicle You flight or you drive.
Runway Usage Determine which runway pair get used most. Left first, right last. Must supply as many values as number of alligned runways. [0.75, 0.25]: Left gets used more than right.
Taxi Hold How long a plane hold a taxi position before entering the runway [60, 300]: Between 60 and 360 seconds.
Take-off Hold How long a plane hold takeoff position on the runway before actually taking off [10, 60]: Between 10 and 70 seconds.
Arrival delay How long ahead / late a plane arrives accoring to its scheduled time. [-20, 50]: Between 20 minutes in advance and 30 minutes late.
Departure (additionnal) delay How long ahead / late a plane leaves accoring to its scheduled time. [-10, 30]: Between 15 minutes in advance and 30 minutes late. If the planed arrived late, its delay is ADDED to this extra delay.
AODB preannouce How long ahead a scheduled flight is announced. 360 minutes.
AODB planned flight time announcement. Time when Planned flight arrival/departure time is announced. [40, 20]: Planned arrival/departure time is announced between 40Â±20 minutes before actual arrival/departure time.
AODB planned flight time announcement. Time ADDED to the above planned flight time announcement. This randomness represent "delays" added by external computers to provide the information. 600: Up to 600 seconds are added to above time.
Passenger / Cargo Flight Ratio Ratio 0.2: 20 percents of flights are PASSENGER (PAX) flights. The other 1 - 0.2 = 0.8 = 80% flights are CARGO flights.
Jitter Omnidirectional "noise" added to GPS coordinates, in meter. 10: Meters. GPS coordinates are sent with that amount of uncertainty added to them, in all, random direction.
Time between flights Time, in minutes, between flights. [0,15]: There are always between 0 and 15 minutes between flights.
Minimum time between flights If random selected time is lower than this, this is the time between 2 flights, never less. 2.5: There are always at least 2.5 minutes between flights.
Turnaround time Time of turnaround for planes that arrive and then leave. [70,120]: Turnaround takes between 70 and 120 minutes to complete.
Parameter for TRUCKS
Loading time Time of unload and load a truck. [35,75]: Unloading and re-loading a truck takes between 35 and 75 minutes to complete.
Time between trucks arriving at dock. Time, in minutes, between arrival of trucks at dock. [2,25]: There are always between 2 and 25 minutes between two trucks arriving at a dock.
